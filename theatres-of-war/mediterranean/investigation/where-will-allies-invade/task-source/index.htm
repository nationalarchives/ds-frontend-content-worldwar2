Where are the Allies going to invade in 1943?

Major Martin of the Royal Marines

Catalogue ref: WO 214/19

This is a photograph of the body of Major Martin of the Royal Marines. His body was washed ashore on the coast of Spain in April 1943. Major Martin was carrying important documents about Allied plans to invade German held Europe in 1943. The Germans did not know where the Allies would invade but the documents Major Martin was carrying appeared to give them vital information.
