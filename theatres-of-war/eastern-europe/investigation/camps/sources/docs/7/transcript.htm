Why didn’t Britain bomb the death camps?

Covering letter from the Jewish Agency for Palestine to the Foreign Office enclosing plans and descriptions of Auschwitz and Treblinka death-camps, August 1944

Catalogue ref: FO 371/42806

THE JEWISH AGENCY FOR PALESTINE

77, GREAT RUSSELL STREET.
LONDON. WC1

18th August, 1944

SECRET

G.E. Millard, Esq.,
Foreign Office,
Whitehall, S.W.1.

Dear Mr. Millard,

As arranged on the telephone this afternoon I enclose herewith plans and descriptions of the two death-camps – at Auschwitz (Oswiecim) and Treblinka (Trzebinia), which I have just received from the Polish Ministry of the Interior.

Yours sincerely,

J.Linton

Enclosures.
