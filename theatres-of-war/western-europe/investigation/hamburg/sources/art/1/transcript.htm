Stettin TO THE RUSSIAN FRONT

Eight times British bombers have flown 13,000 miles to bomb Stettin, the chief Baltic supply port for the German armies in Russia

Stettin was last attacked by the R.A.F. in January 1944. On this and the previous occasion a great force of raiders dropped well over 500 tons of bombs
