Opening Address
3rd September 1943
OBJECTS AND METHODS OF IRREGULAR WARFARE

...

Sporadic risings are useless. Necessity to co-ordinate where possible has produced tabulation [setting out clearly] of United Nations' [Britain, USA and their allies] fundamental objectives in the waging of Irregular Warfare:

  • Politically.
    • To undermine enemy's morale and that of his collaborators.
    • To raise morale of Occupied Territories.
  • Economically.
    • To damage enemy's material.
    • To improve and augment our own material.
      E.g. By infiltration of weapons, explosives, sabotage equipment.
  • Strategically.
    • To damage enemy's manpower and communications.
    • To improve our own manpower and communications.
      E.g. By infiltration of organisers, radio sets and operators, etc.
